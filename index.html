Privacy Policy for BPM Player

Last updated: 07/05/2026

1. Summary

BPM Player is a music playback app that lets you import audio files from your device and play them at adjustable tempo (BPM). We do not run our own servers and we do not collect or store your personal data on our own systems. The only third-party service that processes data is Google AdMob, which we use to display advertising.

2. Data you provide / the App stores locally

  • Audio files and song settings (title, BPM, playlist order) that you import are stored only locally on your device. They are not uploaded to us and are not accessible to us.
  • App settings (colors, pace, preferences) are stored locally on your device.

We have no access to any of the above.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertising through Google AdMob, a service provided by Google. To deliver ads, Google may collect and process certain information, including:

  • Device identifiers (e.g. advertising identifier / IDFA)
  • IP address and approximate location derived from it
  • Information about your device and how you interact with ads
  • Usage and diagnostic data

This data is used to serve, measure and (with your consent) personalize ads. Google's handling of this data is described here:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services
  • Google Advertising

4. Consent (EU/EEA, UK, Switzerland) and App Tracking Transparency

  • On first launch, and where legally required, the App shows a consent form (via Google's User Messaging Platform) asking whether you agree to personalized advertising. If you do not consent, you will only see non-personalized ads.
  • On iOS, the App also shows Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. If you decline tracking, ads will be non-personalized.
  • You can change your choice at any time through your device settings and, where offered, through the App's privacy options.

5. Legal basis (GDPR)

Where the GDPR applies, we process advertising-related data based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw your consent at any time with effect for the future.

6. Data retention

We do not store your personal data ourselves. Data processed by Google for advertising is retained according to Google's own policies (see the links in Section 3).

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to the processing of your personal data, and the right to data portability. To exercise these rights regarding data processed by Google, please refer to Google's privacy resources. For any request to us, contact us using the details above.

8. Children

The App is not directed to children under the age required by law in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
